Brief Background & Overview

  • Island located right under Malaysia
  • Singapore used to be part of the British Crown Colony, then became part of the Malaysia Republic
  • In 1965, Singapore separated from Malaysia and became its own country.
  • Official languages spoke: Mandarin 35%, English 23%, Malay 14.1%, Hokkien 11.4%, Cantonese 5.7%, Teochew 4.9%, Tamil 3.2%, other Chinese dialects 1.8%, other 0.9%
